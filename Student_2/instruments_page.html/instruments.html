Keyboard

A keyboard instrument is a musical instrument played using a keyboard, a row of levers which are pressed by the fingers. The most common of these are the piano, organ, and various electronic keyboards, including synthesizers and digital pianos.

String

A string instrument is a musical instrument that produces sound from vibrating strings when the performer plays or sounds the strings in some manner. String instruments are the most widespread category of musical instruments. They include the instruments with the highest pitch ranges, the violin family, as well as the instruments with the lowest ranges, the double bass family.

Woodwind

Woodwind instruments are a family of musical instruments within the greater category of wind instruments. They are distinguished from other wind instruments by the use of a wooden mouthpiece and a flared bell. The most common woodwind instruments are the flute, oboe, clarinet, bassoon, and saxophone.

Brasswind

Brass instruments are a family of musical instruments in the hornbills subfamily of the woodwind family. They are commonly made of brass and include the instruments most commonly associated with the group, such as the trumpet, horn, tuba, and French horn.

Percussion

Percussion instruments are a class of musical instruments that produce sound from being struck or scraped by a beater, struck, scraped or rubbed by hand, or struck against another similar instrument. The percussion section of an orchestra most commonly contains instruments such as timpani, snare drum, bass drum, cymbals, triangle and tambourine.
